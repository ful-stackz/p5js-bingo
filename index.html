How to play?

Basic

Load and play!

The best gameplay has been already chosen for you (every 5 seconds a number will be picked up to a maximum of 25 numbers). Click the Play button and have fun!

Advanced

Load and play!

If you don't enjoy the game with the default settings then you are free to pick any of the 3 options for each setting. Choose the maximum numbers picked by clicking on the squares with 20, 25 and 30 inside. You can also choose the time interval on which a number is being picked - all you have to do is click on one of the squares with 2.5, 5 and 7.5 inside. This will change the picking interval to 2.5, 5 or 7 seconds respectively. Set it, click the Play button and have fun!
